Sweet Potatoe Pie

Description

The best Sweet potato pie that you will ever taste

Ingredients

  • 1 Large or 2 medium-sized sweet potatoes
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 1/3 Can Borden's sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 Tbsp McCormick Pure Vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 stick of Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Boil sweet potatoes until soft all the way through
  3. Peel sweet potatoesand place in large bowl for mixing
  4. Beat sweet potatoes with electric mixer frequently rinsing mixing paddles until all "strings" are removed
  5. Add Vanilla extract, eggs, and condensed milk and mix together
  6. Add sugar and butter and continue to mix until no lumps remain
  7. Pour sweet potato mix evenly into frozen pie shell
  8. Bake for 35 minutes
  9. Cool and enjoy!
